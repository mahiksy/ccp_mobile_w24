Gheymeh

Gheymeh is a popular traditional dish in Iran. It serves with rice usually or you can substitude the rice with bread

Ingredients

  • Onion: 2 large
  • Red meat: 1 lb
  • Cotyledons: 0.5 lb
  • Tomato paste: 2 Tbsp
  • Water: 2 cups
  • Oil: 5 Tbsp
  • Spices: tumeric, black pepper, cinemon, salt

Steps

  1. Chop the onions and fry them in a pot with oil
  2. Add the spices to the pot once onions are fried
  3. Add the red meat to the pot and stir it for 5 minutes
  4. add the Cotyledons and water to the pot and let it boil for 1.5 on meduim heat
  5. fry your tomato paste for 2 minutes with oil and add to the pot

What makes it better

One thing that makes this food super delicous is french fires. If you like to add as a side for Ghemyeh, that would make this food complete.

Potato Kotlet

Gheymeh is a popular traditional dish in Iran. It serves with rice usually or you can substitude the rice with bread

Ingredients

  • Onion: 1 Meduim
  • potato: 3 meduim
  • eggs: 3 large
  • Oil: 5 Tbsp
  • Spices: tumeric, black pepper, garlic powder, salt

Steps

  1. shred you onion and squeeze the juice out of it and add the rest to a bowl
  2. shred your potato into the bowl
  3. crack the eggs in the bowl and add all the spices
  4. Mix all the ingredients well.
  5. heat up the pan and add your oil. add the batter to the pan make the surface smooth. put the lid on and let it cook for 10 minutes.
  6. then flip the kotlet and let the other side fried for 7 minutes

What makes it better

Potato kotlet is really good with yogurt, ketchup, and pickles.

Herbs Kotlet

Gheymeh is a popular traditional dish in Iran. It serves with rice usually or you can substitude the rice with bread

Ingredients

  • Onion: 1 Meduim
  • chopped herbs: 0.75 lb
  • eggs: 3 large
  • Oil: 5 Tbsp
  • chopped walnuts: 3 Tbsp
  • Spices: tumeric, black pepper, garlic powder, salt

Steps

  1. Add the chopped herbs into a bowl and add the spices and chooped walnuts
  2. crack the eggs inside the bowl and mix everything together
  3. heat up the pan and add your oil. Add the batter to the pan make the surface smooth. Put the lid on and let it cook for 10 minutes.
  4. then flip the kotlet and let the other side fried for 7 minutes

What makes it better

Herb kotlet is really good with Mayo, Yogurt, and Feta cheese
